About Us.

ABOUT THE


COMPANY.

Union Steel Industries is a part of Majeed & Sons Steels (Pvt) Ltd. which was founded in year 1980 in pursuit of dream for a prosperous and developed country. Being a renowned name in the Steel Bars manufacturing industry, believing in continuous modernization, innovation and supply of quality Steel products has enabled future investment in setting up our second new fully Automatic state of art Steel Bar Mill, Union Steel Industries (USI) with production capacity of more than 150,000 mt per annum, with both mills accumulating to above more than 200,000 mt per annum located in the industrial hub of SITE, Karachi.

Alhamdullilah, with combined production, we will be one of the largest steel producer in Karachi with over 250 employees and work force. Union Steel Industries is only steel bar plant in Karachi which uses 100% hot rolled billets of actual grade 60 standards with optimum level of carbon & manganese level as parent raw material, with lowest impurities & elongation greater than 14% without TMT.

OUR COMPETITIVE EDGE


WHAT WE DO.

Union Steel Industries is regarded as a complete solution provider when it comes to steel. Our one window operation provides steel products in all sizes, according to any specification and different grades of Steel under one roof. We are one of the leading Manufacturers of Deformed Steel, TMT and Twisted Steel Bars in addition to being the largest Stockists of Imported Deform Steel Bars in Pakistan as well local agents of leading International Mills.

Union Steel Industries is the only steel bars plant in Karachi using 100% imported Hot Rolled Billets, grade 60 standards (with optimum level of carbon & manganese level) as a parent raw material.

All products are rolled exclusively from Hot Rolled Imported Billets confirming to Grade 60 standards, manufactured by recognized Blast furnaces only, which use IRON ORE and NOT WASTE METAL SCRAP based furnaces which is used mostly by other local integrated plants for manufacturing Billet while the ultimate result is: Premium Quality Steel Bars.
